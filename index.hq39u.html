TIFF 2017 Lineup: New Films from Aronofsky, del Toro, Malick, and More

The initial TIFF 2017 lineup has been announced. The first wave of 2017's Toronto Film Festival's lineup was announced today, and it's one of the strongest in TIFF's history. The slate features premieres from festival stalwarts Terrence Malick, Guillermo del Toro, Darren Aronofsky, Martin McDonagh, Alexander Payne, and even directorial debuts from the likes of actors Andy Serkis and Greta Gerwig.
