How 3 Women Made Estonia's 'Fargo' in 6 Weeks

Meet Fargo's European counterpart: Mother, a new crime thriller from Estonia. Directed by Kadri Kousaar , Mother exposes the vermin beneath the quaint veneer of life in a small town. It's a quietly thrilling whodunit that demonstrates mastery of nuanced character humor. Like Fargo, it deals in absurdity and lurches toward a grim twist.
