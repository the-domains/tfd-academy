'BuyBust': Film Review

Among the recent batch of Philippine movies about the Southeast Asian country's so-called war on drugs, BuyBust is a rare beast: It actually invites the viewer to root for cops who shoot, stab, pummel and behead people in a poverty-stricken shantytown. But director Erik Matti is no cheerleader for populist president Rodrigo Duterte and his advocacy of extrajudicial violence.
