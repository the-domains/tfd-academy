Garfield wins best leading play actor Tony Award

NEW YORK - Andrew Garfield has won the Tony Award for best leading actor in a play for his work in "Angels in America," Tony Kushner's monumental drama about life and love during the 1980s. Garfield plays a young gay man living with AIDS in the sprawling, seven-hour revival opposite Nathan Lane.

'Ocean's 8' opens with franchise-best $41.5M to top weekend | Philstar.com

NEW YORK - "Ocean's 8," the female-fronted overhaul of the starry heist franchise, opened with an estimated $41.5 million at the box office, taking the weekend's top spot from the fast-falling "Solo: A Star Wars Story."
